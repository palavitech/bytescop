Help
Engagement Details
View engagement details and manage the assessment.
  • Client: The client this engagement belongs to.
  • Status: Current phase — Planned, Active, On Hold, or Completed.
  • Timeline: Shows days remaining or overdue based on start/end dates.
  • Description: Summary of the engagement scope and objectives.
  • Notes: Internal notes for the team.
  • Findings: View and manage security findings for this engagement.
  • Visualize: Toggles a chart card showing findings by severity, status, and asset breakdown.
  • Report: Generate a PDF report of the engagement and its findings.
  • Settings: Configure engagement-level settings, team members, and stakeholders.
  • Edit: Update the engagement details.
  • Delete: Permanently remove the engagement and its findings.
  • Statement of Work: The SoW is auto-created with the engagement. Edit it to set a title, approve it to lock scope, and manage in-scope assets.
