Help
New Engagement Wizard
Create a new engagement in 5 easy steps:
  • Step 1: Choose or create the client.
  • Step 2: Select assets that will be in scope, or create new ones.
  • Step 3: Fill in the engagement details (name, dates, description).
  • Step 4: Review the auto-generated Statement of Work and approve it.
  • Step 5: Review everything and activate the engagement.
Clients and assets created during this wizard are saved immediately, even if you don't complete the engagement.
