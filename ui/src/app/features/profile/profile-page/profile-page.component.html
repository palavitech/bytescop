Help
Profile
Manage your personal profile information and avatar image.
  • Avatar: Upload a profile image (PNG, JPEG, GIF, WebP, max 2 MB). Images are automatically resized to 256x256 pixels.
  • Name: Update your first and last name. Changes appear immediately in the navigation bar.
  • Phone / Timezone: Optional contact details. Phone and timezone are shown in engagement team members and stakeholders.
  • Account: Email, role, and membership date are read-only and managed by your administrator.
  • Password: Change your password here. The live checklist shows your organization's password policy requirements. All checks must pass before you can submit. If MFA is enabled, your authenticator code is also required.
  • Password Expiry: If your organization enforces password expiry, you'll see the days remaining. Expired passwords must be changed before you can continue using the app.
  • MFA: Enable, disable, or change your authenticator device. When enabled, you'll need your authenticator code for login and sensitive actions. Download and save your backup codes in case you lose your device.
